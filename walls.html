Wall Jumping

Wall jumping is a powerful technique that lets you bounce off vertical surfaces by aiming rockets at walls during a jump. Mastering this skill opens up advanced map traversal and allows you to redirect your flight path in midair.

Technique Breakdown:

  • Approach a wall at an angle and fire a rocket at it while midair.
  • Use crouch-jumping to reduce distance from wall without losing height.
  • Reposition with air-strafing after the explosion.
